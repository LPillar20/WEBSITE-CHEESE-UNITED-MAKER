HISTORY ABOUT US

Started at 1919 after the WWI, The CHEESE UNITED MAKER Was founded by Mike OxMaul. CHEESE UNITED MAKER Is a group of people that dedicate their life make cheese for a living. They were loved by the people during the great deppresion for selling cheap and high quality cheese. After the deppresion the CHEESE UNITED MAKER did a campaign across the world to feed the starving. The campaign is still going to this day under the command of the owner Mike OxMaul, and Mike Oxlong

OUR GOALS

- Feed the unfortunate - Searching the best cheese maker - Have fun and experience -

CHEESE UNITED MAKER Are happy to accept skilled cheese maker who hate seeing starving children
